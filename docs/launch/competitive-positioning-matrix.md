# SMIRK Competitive Positioning Matrix

Research date: 2026-07-16

Use this as the operating filter for launch copy, pricing, proof assets, and channel choices. Recheck source pages before changing pricing claims.

## Positioning Decision

SMIRK should compete as missed-call recovery for home-service businesses, not as a generic AI receptionist or developer voice-agent platform.

Primary wedge:
- Dedicated recovery number or missed-call capture.
- Owner alert.
- Callback task.
- Dashboard proof.
- Clear Starter entry price.

What to avoid:
- Staff replacement framing.
- Unsupported recovered-revenue promises.
- Unlimited voice or SMS.
- Developer-platform positioning.
- Cold SMS or automated phone-spam acquisition.

## Net Decision

Adopt:
- Smith.ai trust packaging.
- Goodcall-style buying simplicity.
- Product Hunt launch assets.
- G2/Capterra credibility setup.
- Usage caps learned from infrastructure platforms.

Reject for this sprint:
- Generic receptionist positioning.
- Developer-platform messaging.
- Unlimited or lifetime voice/SMS offers.
- Broad paid spend before proof.
- SMS-first or cold-SMS growth.
